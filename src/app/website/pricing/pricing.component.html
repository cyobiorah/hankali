Frequently asked questions

  • What is Eluzini Syinin?

    Eluzini Syinin is a gatekeeping solution that simplifies and automates the visitor management and employee clock-in processes of organisations, providing real-time updates and instant notification on all activities at your front desk across multiple locations.

  • What do I need to use Eluzini?

    This is depends on your organisations IT policy and preferred deployment method.
    Eluzini can be deployed on servers- on-premise or cloud service platform like Heroku, Azure, Amazon Web Service etc.
    To access the gate keeping features like visitor and employee check-in, this can be achieved via:

    1. Web App through web browser on www.eluzini.com
    2. Eluzini Welcome tablet app: Android devices must be on Android Marshmallow 6.0 upwards. Elzuini Welcome is coming soon to iOS.

  • How do your plans work?

    Eluzini Essential and Professional plans starts off with 100 employees for $100 per month, additional 100 employees costs $50. For example Color Pallet Ltd has 300 employees, they will be paying $200 a month- $100 for the initial 100 employees and $100 for the additional 200 employees (that is $50 for each additional 100 employees).

    Eluzini Enterprise Essential is a one time payment option that offers on premise server or self hosted cloud deployment, for more details contact sales@eluzini.com or call +2348157033850.

  • What hardware is required to work with Eluzini?

    To utilize Eluzini Welcome tablet app and Eluzini Alert mobile app, you require an Android tablet and phone on Android Marshmallow 6.0 upwards. Elzuini Alert and Welcome are only avaiable on Android but will be coming soon to Apple iOS.

  • What Printer do i need to work with Eluzini?

    For seamless integration with Eluzini Welcome, Brother QL820 is the recommended label printer.

  • How do I know when I have a Visitor?

    You will be alerted by email, SMS if configured (this is an additional service), via Web app and Eluzini Alert app.

  • Can I restrict who can visit me?

    Yes, Eluzini provides visitor approval workflow which restricts permitting just anyone into your office and also helps reduce waste of visitor badges. Also after reviewing a visitors information via the web app or Eluzini Alert app, hosts can inform receptionist and security on next steps via Conversations feature, the instant messaging and notification center of Eluzini.
